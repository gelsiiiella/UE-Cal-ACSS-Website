Exciting Gaming Tournament Announcement!

We are thrilled to announce an exhilarating gaming tournament organized by our esteemed organization. This tournament will feature two of the most popular and competitive games in the eSports scene: Teamfight Tactics (TFT) and Tekken 8.

We have limited slots for participants to ensure a high-quality and manageable event. For Teamfight Tactics, we are accepting only 24 participants, and for Tekken 8, we are limiting it to 32 participants. This is to ensure that every participant gets a fair and equal chance to showcase their skills and strategies.

Tekken Participants are also allowed to bring their own controllers, ensuring they can play at their best with the equipment they are most comfortable with.

We look forward to seeing the exciting matches and memorable moments this tournament will surely bring. Let the games begin!
